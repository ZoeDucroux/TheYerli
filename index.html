the Yerli.

Here to help our nation’s small business entrepreneurs

The Yerli was founded with a vision to support local entrepreneurs in every community along their difficult journeys. We understand that they have poured everything they have to create their vision at the expense of their life savings, relationships, and sweat equity. An entire portion of the U.S. economy exists to support these entrepreneurs, but driven by profits have forgotten who they were founded to help. We want to bring the focus back on our entrepreneurs, now and always.

Here to help communities reconnect in a disconnected world

Having recently encountered a novel virus that led to a global pandemic and to social disconnect, disconnection has become the new normal. What is needed to regain normality is not only a way to virtually connect, but to create a sense of affiliation and community. To join something bigger than each individual that can change the lives of humans on a personal scale: collective-personal connection. The Yerli is aiming to reconnect people to normalcy by mending what built people in the start: their communities. Although it seems like the world has changed forever, we can and will return to a better future, The Yerli just wants to help play its part in catalyzing that.
